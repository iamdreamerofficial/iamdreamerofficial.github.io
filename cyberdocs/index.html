Hacking partners

We are using below operating Systems to teach about the real-world hacking.

Kali Linux

Offensive Security

Kali Linux comes pre-installed with a vast collection of powerful hacking tools and utilities that make it an essential platform for anyone involved in the field of cybersecurity. Whether you're a professional penetration tester or a beginner just starting out.

BlackArch Linux

BlackArch Linux . Org

BlackArch Linux is designed to be lightweight, fast, and customizable, making it an ideal platform for penetration testing and security research. It is designed to be used by security professionals who need a powerful and flexible operating system to test their networks and systems against real-world attacks.

Parrot OS

ParrotSec . Org

Parrot OS is an open-source operating system, which means that it is freely available to download and use. It has a large and active community of users and developers. We invite you to explore the operating system and join our community of cybersecurity professionals and enthusiasts.
